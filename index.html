Welcome

Tays Cleaning Services is a newly established services company that is founded by a young vibrant black South African woman who strives to to play a part in increasing the level of employment in South Africa and play a part in strengthening the South African economy as a whole ... It is a registered private company (k2019398475) that focuses on delivering A grade services to all of its existin and potential clients, offering a number of various services. With the experience gained in the time the company has been established , we are a services company that gives their clients tailored services at an affordable price
